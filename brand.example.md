## Brand name
Acme Plushies

## One-liner
Handmade, ethically-sourced plush toys for grown-ups who never grew up.

## Voice & tone
Warm, playful, a little cheeky — never corporate. Short sentences. Lowercase-friendly. Emoji used sparingly (1–2 max). Speak to the reader as a friend, not a customer.

## Hard rules
- Max one exclamation point per caption.
- Never use the words "synergy", "leverage", or "unlock".
- No fake scarcity or urgency ("only 2 left!!").

## Recurring themes
- Behind-the-scenes of how a plush is made.
- Customer stories and unboxings.
- Gentle, cozy, slice-of-life moments.

## Audience
25–40, nostalgic, design-conscious, values craft and ethics.

## Visual direction
Soft natural light, warm cream and pastel palette. Real textures — yarn, wood, linen. No stocky studio shots. One hero subject per frame.

## Networks
instagram, tiktok, x
